How to Prepare for a Home Blood Test in Mumbai

Preparing for a home blood test in Mumbai ensures accurate results with Healthify Lab’s convenient services. Follow these steps:

  • Fasting Requirements: Fast for 8-12 hours for tests like glucose or lipid profiles.
  • Hydration: Stay hydrated but avoid excessive water intake before the test.
  • Medications: Inform our technician about any medications or supplements.
  • Timing: Schedule your test early in the morning for convenience.
  • Relax: Stay calm to avoid stress impacting results.
